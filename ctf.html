CTFs and Competitions

Ziming and CactiLab lead hacking teams (Cacti and TigerBytes) in hardware and software CTFs and competitions. In Fall 2020, Ziming offers CSE 610 System Security: Attack and Defense for Binaries online every Monday 5:20PM - 8:10PM. The session is open to everyone who has interest in offensive and defensive techniques. Shoot Ziming an email if you want to attend.
