CTFs and Competitions

Prof. Zhao and CactiLab lead hacking teams (Cacti and TigerBytes) in hardware and software CTFs and competitions. In Fall 2019, Prof. Zhao runs a public hacking training session every Thursday 2:30PM - 4:30PM at GOL-2500. The session is open to everyone who has interest in offensive and defensive techniques.

Team TigerBytes (in the picture from left to right: Thomas Cenova, Brandon Adler, and Eric Scheler) advised by Dr. Ziming Zhao and Dr. Marcin Lukowiak placed third in the 2019 MITRE Collegiate eCTF (embedded capture-the-flag) cybersecurity competition.
